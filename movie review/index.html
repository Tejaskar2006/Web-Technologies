Review: RRR

Synopsis

S.S. Rajamouli's "RRR" (Rise, Roar, Revolt) is a cinematic tour de force, a bombastic and exhilarating mythological action-drama that has captivated audiences worldwide. The film is a fictionalized account of two real-life Indian revolutionaries, Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju, imagining a friendship between them before they became historical legends. With breathtaking action sequences, a powerful emotional core, and stellar performances from its leads, "RRR" is a cinematic experience that demands to be seen on the biggest screen possible.
